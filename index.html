Who I Am

About Me

Building smarter, sustainable energy solutions for tomorrow

I'm a results-driven Electrical Engineer specializing in power systems, renewable energy integration, and EV charging infrastructure. With a strong foundation in control systems, IoT applications, and harmonic analysis, I bring both theoretical depth and practical experience to every project.

My internship at Ashuganj Power Station Company Limited (APSCL) gave me hands-on exposure to large-scale power plant operations — analyzing turbine KPIs, proposing maintenance schedules, and understanding grid-level safety protocols. This real-world experience deeply shaped my interest in the energy sector.

As an Undergraduate Researcher at HSTU, I conducted thesis research on EV charging station grid stability — designing MATLAB/Simulink models to analyze voltage fluctuations, harmonic distortion, and transformer loading, with findings on microgrid stability and renewable energy integration.

Thesis: Grid Stabilization for EV Charging Stations — modeling LCL filters and Front-End Converters (FEC) to mitigate grid disturbances and improve power quality.
